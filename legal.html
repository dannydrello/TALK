Child Safety Standards

Last updated: May 2026

This Child Safety Standards Policy applies to TALK and is maintained by Talk Wide Technologies Ltd.

Zero Tolerance Statement

TALK strictly prohibits Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE), including Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), grooming, solicitation of minors, trafficking, and any activity that sexually exploits children.

1. Commitment to Child Safety

TALK is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, or harmful behavior involving minors.

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

Users are strictly prohibited from:

  • Creating, sharing, or promoting any content that involves child sexual abuse or exploitation
  • Engaging in conversations or interactions that are sexual in nature with minors
  • Attempting to groom, manipulate, or exploit individuals under the age of 18
  • Impersonating minors for harmful purposes

Any violation of these rules will result in immediate action, including account suspension or permanent removal.

3. Reporting and Enforcement

TALK provides mechanisms for users to report harmful or suspicious behavior.

  • Reports can be made through in-app reporting tools or official communication channels
  • All reports are reviewed promptly and taken seriously
  • Verified violations will lead to:
    • Immediate account termination and removal of offending content
    • Reporting to law enforcement authorities when necessary
    • Cooperation with investigations to ensure the safety of minors

4. Cooperation with Authorities

TALK will cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and regulatory bodies in the investigation of child exploitation or abuse cases.

5. To ensure safety on the platform, TALK may implement:

To ensure safety on the platform, TALK may implement:

  • Automated content monitoring to detect and remove harmful content
  • Age verification processes to prevent underage access
  • Restrictions on interactions where necessary

6. User Responsibility

All users are expected to:

  • Use the platform in a manner that does not harm others
  • Respect the rights and dignity of all users
  • Report any suspicious or harmful behavior they encounter
  • Avoid engaging in or supporting harmful behavior
  • Report any content or activity that may endanger minors
  • Respect community standards

7. Policy Updates

TALK reserves the right to update this policy at any time to reflect evolving safety standards and legal requirements.
