Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

TALK is the ultimate student platform that connects campus life, lifestyle, and opportunities all in one place. From conversations to marketplaces, events to communities, TALK keeps you engaged and connected to what matters most at your university.

To create an account, download the TALK app, click "Sign Up," enter your university email address, set a password, and complete your profile. University email verification is required to join your campus community.

TALK is expanding to universities worldwide. Check our website to see if your campus is currently available. If your campus isn't listed yet, you can sign up to be notified when we launch.

TALK is primarily designed for your university community. However, you can connect with students from other universities through our global opportunities section in Horizon.

Account & Security

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen, enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data. Your privacy is our priority. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You can contact support if you need immediate deletion.

Navigate to Settings > Privacy. Here you can control who sees your profile, posts, and activity. You can customize these settings at any time.

Campus Market

Go to Campus Market, click "Sell Item," add photos, title, description, and price. Review your listing and publish. Your item will be visible to your campus community immediately.

TALK takes a small commission on completed sales to maintain the platform. The fee is deducted from your sale price. Check Campus Market for current fee details.

Browse items, click on a listing, and click "Buy Now" or "Make Offer." Complete the payment, arrange pickup/delivery with the seller, and rate the transaction when complete.

If there's an issue with a purchase, contact the seller first. If unresolved, go to your transaction and click "Report Issue." Our support team will review and help resolve it.

Features & Usage

Open Billboard and browse trending posts and announcements. Use filters to find specific categories. You can also follow clubs and organizations to see their events.

Visit Horizon and use the search filters by field, company, location, and deadline. Set up job alerts to get notified about new opportunities matching your preferences.
